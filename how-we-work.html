How We Work


Follow our simple five step process and let us help you solve your property problems, quickly and smoothly.

Please note, if you don’t want to sell, we can still help you. Simply get in touch for a friendly chat.

Step One

Fill your details in our offer form here, or ring 07753 836 033 to see how we can help.

Step Two

Assuming you are looking for a quick sale, after filling in our offer form, we will call you within 24 hours to discuss your circumstances and find out more. At this point, we can normally find a solution and make an offer “in principle.”

Step Three

If you are happy with the proposed solution, and you accept our offer “in principle” we will visit your house and make you a written offer.

Step Four

If you accept, we will commit to buy your property quickly (or to your timescale), easily and with no stress for you.

Step Five

You can choose when you would like to complete on a date that suits you. You are under no pressure.


It couldn’t be easier. This process is 100% confidential, and we will work with you to help make the process simple and hassle free.
